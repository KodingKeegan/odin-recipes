#1 Best Cereal Recipe

Brief overview of the #1 Best Cereal Recipe

This quick and easy breakfast meal takes minutes to prep, and is absolutly to die for. This recipe is composed of a total of four base ingredients!

List of ingredients Required
  • Handmade Stoneware Ceramic Bowl
  • 24k Gold-Plated Dinner Size Spoon
  • Arabaian Grass-Fed 2% Whole Milk
  • Box of Kellogs Grade Fruitloops
Steps to Crafting Perfection
  1. Firmly Grasp Fruitloops and pour into Ceramic Bowl
  2. Add Grass-Fed 2% Milk atop of Fruitloops
  3. Let Contents of Ceramic Bowl set for 1min 30sec
  4. Insert 24k Dinner Size Spoon into bowl and enjoy
