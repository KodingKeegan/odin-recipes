World Famous Grilled-Cheese Recipe

Brief overview of the #1 Best Grilled-Cheese Recipe

Who knew such an artesinal dish, which thrives on the edge of near prefection critics would say, is comprised of three main ingredients

List of ingredients Required
  • Kraft Sliced Cheese
  • Freshly Churned Grass-Fed Butter
  • Whole-Wheat GMO Certified Bread
Steps to Crafting Perfection
  1. Set flat pan to medium heat, add 1/4 stick of butter
  2. Rub sliced bread atop of melted butter until glistening yellow
  3. stack ingredents vertical, accordingly:
  4. Bread slice
  5. Kraft Cheese Slice
  6. Another Kraft Cheese Slice
  7. One More Kraft Cheese Slice
  8. Add The Remaining Kraft Cheese Slice(s)
  9. Bread Slice
  10. Place Sandwich into Pan
  11. Fry on Medium heat, 2min each side
  12. After 4min of frying, remove cheese vessul and enjoy
